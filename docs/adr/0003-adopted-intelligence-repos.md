# ADR 0003: Adopted Intelligence Repos

## Decision

Prepare adapters for LangGraph, TurboVec, pgvector, Graphiti, Cognee, Mem0,
LiteLLM, MCP, Langfuse, Promptfoo, and Ragas.

## Consequence

AION can adopt strong repositories without surrendering its Brain contracts.
These systems are implementation engines only and must remain behind adapters.

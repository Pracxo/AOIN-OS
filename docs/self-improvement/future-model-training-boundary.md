# Future Model Training Boundary

AION-175 does not authorize model-weight training.

## Boundary

The self-improvement platform may record evaluation outcomes, learning outcomes, preference distributions, strategy statistics, retrieval-ranking candidates, planning cases, and data-only procedural skill records. These records are operational evidence and candidate policy data. They are not model-weight updates.

## Prohibited Without Future Authorization

- Fine-tuning or training model weights.
- Exporting protected holdout content into model-training material.
- Using approval evidence to weaken safety gates.
- Treating adaptive candidates as source-code changes.
- Activating learned preferences for high-impact behavior without approval.

Any future model-training work must be proposed as a separate program with its own authorization, privacy review, security review, benchmark boundary, rollback model, and operator approval.

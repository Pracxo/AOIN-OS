# Knowledge Intelligence Research Source Policy

Source classification is metadata. Classification does not prove a claim. A source marked primary may still contain an error. A source marked community_unverified or unknown cannot independently establish a verified fact. Disallowed sources must fail closed.

## Authorized Source Classes

- primary_authoritative
- official_standard
- official_government
- peer_reviewed
- vendor_primary
- institutional_primary
- reputable_secondary
- community_unverified
- unknown
- disallowed

AION-205 must not implement the final truth-verification decision. That belongs to AION-211.
